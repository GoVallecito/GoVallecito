Living in Vallecito · Firewise Living

Living Firewise in fire country

Fire is part of life in the San Juans — the 2002 Missionary Ridge Fire burned more than 70,000 acres right around the lake (the chainsaw carvings on the Tour of Carvings memorialize those trees). Here is how to harden a home, create defensible space, and stay ready — tied to our live fire-restriction status.
