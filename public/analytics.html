How this works

Measurement & reporting

What we measure & why

Visits & pageviews — overall reach. Top pages — what pulls people in (the conditions dashboard and fishing lead). Referrers & search terms — how people discover us, and the intent behind it. Partner profile views & actions — the value each listing delivers. Devices & geography — who and how.

The tools (free, privacy-first)

Cloudflare Web Analytics — cookie-free human visits, pageviews, referrers, countries, devices (no consent banner needed). Google Search Console — the actual search terms surfacing the site and their clicks/impressions. First-party action tracking — a lightweight, no-cookie counter that records taps on partner call/website/booking buttons so we can report "actions sent" per listing.

Reporting cadence

A monthly one-page report per Featured Partner (views, actions, top days), plus this live dashboard for the whole-site picture. Quarterly, a short trend summary for renewals and outreach.

Definitions & honesty notes

A visit is a real person arriving from outside the site — not the same as a request (every image, script, and the 15-minute conditions refresh is a request, and crawlers/bots add many more). The "traffic by country" panel above is request data and includes bots, which is why it shows heavy datacenter countries; human visits are tracked separately and are overwhelmingly U.S./local. We never invent numbers or reviews.
