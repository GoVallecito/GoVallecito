Stay aware

Smoke and closures can change fast in fire season. Check before you tow a boat up, and have an exit plan — there's effectively one paved route in (CR 501); the Florida Road / CR 240 way past Lemon Reservoir is a slower backcountry alternate.

Lake level

Vallecito Reservoir

Storage and pool elevation from USACE CWMS (USBR water-operations data), with USBR RISE as a fallback — percent-of-full against the reservoir's ~129,700 acre-foot capacity. (The old USGS reservoir gauge 09353000 is stale and no longer used.) PRID publishes levels too.

What it means

Low water pulls ramps and changes where fish hold; high water after a big snow year can cover the swim beach at the north end. The marina (970) 884-7000 has the day-to-day read.

Winter note

CR 501 climbs and can ice over before US-160 does. In storms, carry chains or run good snow tires — and check before an early-morning fishing run.
