Pine River Lodge

Cabins and lodge rooms right on the Pine River — a Vallecito mainstay for families and anglers.

On the river, in the trees

Pine River Lodge sits right where the Pine River runs into the north end of Vallecito Lake — walking distance to the water, the trailheads, and the quiet end of the valley. A mix of cabins and lodge rooms makes it a fit for couples, families, and fishing crews alike.

📞(970) 884-2563 🌐pineriverlodge.com 📍Vallecito Lake, Bayfield, CO
Book / check availability → Ask GoVallecito

Why stay here

  • Location. North end of the lake, close to the marina and the Vallecito Creek / Pine River trailheads.
  • Range of rooms. Cabins for groups, lodge rooms for a simpler stay.
  • Four-season. Summer boating and fishing, fall color, winter quiet.

Details, photos, and rates are maintained by the lodge — confirm current availability on pineriverlodge.com or by phone.
