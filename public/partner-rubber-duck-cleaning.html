Rubber Duck Cleaning

"Leaving it squeaky clean." A local, family-run cleaning crew that turns stressful, cluttered spaces into fresh, spotless ones — homes, rentals, RVs, offices, and new builds across Vallecito, Bayfield, Durango, and Ignacio.

The short version

A local family that makes the mess disappear

Rubber Duck Cleaning is owned and run by Maria and Brandi, who started small and have grown by word of mouth — realtors, vacation-rental owners, and neighbors who just want to come home to a clean house. They're licensed and insured, serving Vallecito, Bayfield, Durango, Ignacio, and the surrounding areas, and they specialize in the deep, detailed cleans most people would rather not tackle themselves. For big move-outs and post-cleanup jobs they often team up with fellow GoVallecito partner Junk Genies — haul out the junk, then make what's left sparkle.
