Blue Spruce RV Park & Cabins

"A quiet, full-amenity RV park with a few unique twists," nestled in a pine forest that backs up to the mountains — "enter the San Juan National Forest right from your own backdoor." You don't even need to own an RV.

The short version

A wooded basecamp at the lake

Full-hookup RV sites sit among the pines with the national forest right out the back. More than half of Blue Spruce's guests are first-time RV users — which is part of why they offer so many ways to stay without owning a rig: deluxe pre-setup RVs, cabins, and two named apartments, all fully equipped. It runs like a small resort, with a dining hall, game room, country store, and a summer calendar of bingo and live music.

Best features

What sets it apart

🌲 Forest out your back door

Full-hookup 30/50-amp pads in a pine forest — step straight into the San Juan National Forest from your site.

🏕️ No RV? No problem

Pre-setup deluxe RVs, cabins, and two named loft apartments — fully equipped, "ready like a hotel room."

🎶 A social hub

Dining hall, pavilion, game room, country store, two playgrounds — plus weekly bingo and live music all summer.

Ways to stay

Sites, RVs, cabins & apartments

RV sites

Water, sewer, and electric with 30 & 50-amp service; free WiFi (good for email and social, not streaming) plus cable, coin laundry, and clean bathrooms.

Deluxe pre-setup RVs

Sleep 2–6, with a full kitchen, AC/heat, and linens — set up and ready like a hotel room.

Cabins

Sleep up to 7, with kitchens, fire pits, and linens.

Apartments

Two named units — The Ponderosa and The Park Loft.

Glamping

Glamping and tent stays are offered seasonally — ask for current options.

Good to know

Amenities & policies

  • On-site: dining hall, game room with parlor games, outdoor pavilion, country store, coin laundry, two playgrounds, propane fills, horseshoes, and basketball.
  • Summer fun: weekly bingo and live music all season.
  • Pets: welcome on a 6-ft leash and not left unattended; some units are no-pet.
  • Quiet hours: 10 p.m.–7 a.m.
  • Fish cleaning: clean your catch at the marina, not at the park.
  • Deposits & cancellation: per the park's rules page — review before booking.
