How we verify

Where our conditions come from

Every live number on this site is pulled automatically from a public agency feed every 15 minutes — we don't hand-type conditions, and we don't editorialize the data. Here's exactly what feeds what, and what we keep current by hand.

The pipeline

Automatic, every 15 minutes

A scheduled job fetches each source, normalizes it, and serves a single conditions file the site reads. If a source is down, we keep the last good reading and label it — we never invent a value or show a stale number as "now."

Automated vs. curated

What's a feed, and what's a person

🤖 Automated feeds

Weather, alerts, lake level, streamflow, wildfires, and roads are machine feeds from the agencies above. No human edits the numbers; the job just fetches and normalizes them every 15 minutes.

✍️ Locally curated

Fire-restriction stage, the business directory, emergency contacts, and the trip-planning guides are maintained by local contributors and updated as things change. We re-verify contacts before launch and fix mistakes fast.
