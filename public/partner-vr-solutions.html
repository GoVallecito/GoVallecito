VR Solutions

Owner-run construction and remodeling in the Vallecito and Bayfield area — whole-home remodels, bathrooms, decks, steel buildings, excavation, and welding. Led by Aaron VanRiper, with a reputation for clear communication and staying on budget.

Who they are

A contractor neighbors trust

VR Solutions is owned and run by Aaron VanRiper, serving the Vallecito and Bayfield area of Southwest Colorado. Aaron is genuinely hands-on — he'll frame a deck, weld the steel, run the excavator, and tile the shower himself. That range means one crew can take a project from dirt work and structure all the way to the finish carpentry and tile. As one client put it, he "consistently goes above and beyond… communicates clearly throughout the process, and always stays within budget." Got a project in mind? He'll come take a look and give you an honest estimate.

What they do

From dirt work to finish tile

🏡 Remodels & renovations

Whole-home and single-room remodels — kitchens, bathrooms, flooring, and finish carpentry, including full flood-recovery rebuilds.

🚿 Bathrooms & tile

Down-to-the-studs bathrooms with waterproofed, custom-tiled showers and tub surrounds, niches, and new fixtures.

🪵 Decks & outdoor living

Cedar decks, covered porches, and rooftop decks built for the view and the weather.

🏗️ Steel buildings & new construction

Steel-frame shops and garages, additions, and ground-up builds.

Trenching, utilities, drainage, and grading with their own equipment.

🔧 Welding & steel

On-site welding and steel fabrication — railings, beams, and structural work done in-house.
