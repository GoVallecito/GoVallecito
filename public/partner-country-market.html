Vallecito Lake Country Market & La Comida Ranchera

The lake's year-round "one stop shop" — groceries, a made-to-order Mexican kitchen, and a liquor store under one roof, plus gas and propane. On a lake with one road in, that's the difference between a quick stop and a 45-minute run to town.

The short version

Everything you forgot to pack

Three businesses share the building: the grocery, the La Comida Ranchera Mexican kitchen, and Vallecito Liquors. Add gas and propane out front and it's the one place at the lake that covers a forgotten ingredient, a hot dinner, a six-pack, and a fill-up in a single stop — open all year.

Best features

Why locals and visitors lean on it

🛒 A true one-stop

Groceries, hot Mexican food, liquor, gas, and propane — the only one of its kind at the lake, and open year-round.

🌮 La Comida Ranchera

A made-to-order kitchen serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner, with rotating specials.

🍺 Locally stocked liquor

Vallecito Liquors leans local — Ska Brewing, Durango Brewing, and Honeyville Whiskey among the shelves.

What's inside

Under one roof

Grocery

Everyday groceries, ice cream, espresso, beverages, chips, salsa and chilies — plus their own homemade fudge.

La Comida Ranchera (the kitchen)

Made to order, all day: breakfast burritos, tacos, enchiladas, chimichangas, chile relleno, green chili, fajitas, queso, burgers, and take-and-bake pizza, with rotating specials. Dine in, on the patio, or carry out.

Vallecito Liquors

Beer, wine, and spirits with a Four Corners lean — local craft names like Ska Brewing, Durango Brewing, and Honeyville Whiskey.

Fuel & propane

Gas and propane on-site — the convenient fill-up at the lake so you don't have to drive to town.

Good to know

Hours & how to order

  • Hours: Monday 7 a.m.–7 p.m. · Tuesday & Wednesday closed · Thursday–Saturday 7 a.m.–7 p.m. · Sunday 7 a.m.–3 p.m.
  • Open year-round, with a dine-in room and patio.
  • Ways to order: carryout, phone, and curbside.

Hours can shift seasonally — it's worth a quick call or a check of their Facebook before a special trip.
