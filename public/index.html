First time here?

Vallecito at a glance

The quick answers — then dig into Plan your visit for the details.

Can I swim?

Yes — the north end is the spot. No lifeguards, and the water stays snowmelt-cold into June.

Can I bring a boat?

Yes — rentals at the marina, and a PRID recreation permit is required.

Family-friendly?

Very — easy lakeside walks, swimming, and the self-guided Tour of Carvings.

Best season?

Summer for the water; September–October for fishing, fewer crowds, and golden aspens.

Why Vallecito over Durango or Purgatory?

Quieter and uncrowded, wilderness on three sides, and a real lake-town feel at altitude.

How far is the lake?

Vallecito sits in the San Juan Mountains of southwest Colorado, about a half-hour northeast of Durango. Times vary with weather, season, and traffic.
