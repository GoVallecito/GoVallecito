# Photo Wishlist — what to gather (for David)

Both audits agree imagery is the single biggest upgrade. You said you can gather some — here's the
priority shot list and how to drop them in. Your own phone photos are fine if they're sharp and
well-lit; landscape (horizontal) orientation for most.

## How to add them
Drop files into `public/assets/img/` using these exact names. They'll appear automatically; until a file
exists, the site falls back to a tasteful placeholder so nothing breaks.

| Filename | Shot | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| `hero.jpg` | **Sunrise/sunset over the lake** with mountain reflection | The single most important shot. Wide/horizontal, high-res (~2000px wide). Calm morning water is ideal. |
| `lake.jpg` | The lake / a boat or pontoon on the water | Bright, blue-sky day. |
| `fishing.jpg` | An angler on the water or a nice catch | Kokanee, pike, or trout held up; or a rod-on-the-boat shot. |
| `hiking.jpg` | A trail, peak, or aspen-gold hillside | Fall color is gold here — great if you have it. |
| `winter.jpg` | Snowy lake or Nordic trail | Shows the year-round/living angle. |
| `nightsky.jpg` | Milky Way / stars over the lake | You're at altitude with dark skies — a standout if you can get it. |
| `marina.jpg` | The marina / docks | Supports the boating + business content. |
| `town.jpg` | Tour of Carvings, general store, or a local spot | Community character. |
| `gallery-1.jpg` … `gallery-6.jpg` | Any 6 favorites | For the home gallery strip — mix of water, mountains, wildlife, seasons. |

## Tips
- **Horizontal** for hero/section images; square or either for gallery.
- Bigger is better (we can shrink, not enlarge) — aim for the long edge ≥ 1600px.
- Real, local, slightly imperfect beats generic stock — that's your whole brand advantage.
- If you grab wildlife (elk, deer, loons on the lake), even better — drop them into the gallery slots.
- Got photos from locals? Get a quick OK to use them, and we can credit them on the page.

## Optional later
- Drone/aerial of the lake (huge impact if available).
- Short seasonal sets (same view across summer/fall/winter) for the "Living in Vallecito" pages.
