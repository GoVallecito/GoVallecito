## Rules (the ride-legal block, recapped from respect-vallecito/permits hub)
- Get there by trailer: **no La Plata County road is open to OHVs** (C.R.S. 33-14.5-108) — CR 501 is
  off-limits; crossings only.
- CPW registration/permit **$26.25/yr** (Apr 1–Mar 31), residents AND out-of-staters AND plated rigs on
  designated trails; **no out-of-state reciprocity**.
- **The MVUM is the legal map**: Columbine RD 2026 MVUM download fs.usda.gov/media/73572 (georeferenced
  PDF; hard copies no longer printed — download BEFORE you drive up, no cell up there). Riding off
  designated routes = federal violation.
- USFS-approved **spark arrester** required; Colorado limits OHV sound (96 dB on new machines); kids
  10–15 on designated trails only under direct visual supervision; under-18 DOT helmet guidance.
- **Everything past the designated routes is Weminuche Wilderness** — no motors, no bikes, period.
- Open-range cattle + cattle guards; storms move fast above 10,000 ft; season 6/1–11/30.
- Columbine RD (970) 884-2512 (walk-in Fridays 10–4:30) for current gate/conditions. Oct-2025 flood
  damaged roads forest-wide — check fs.usda.gov/r02/sanjuan/conditions.

## The destination story (celebrate-first material)
- **Tuckerville:** born of a 1913 copper-silver rush in the Cave Basin Mining District ("a five-foot
  vein of good copper and galena"), busiest in the 1920s — six bunkhouses, a shop, a mess hall —
  abandoned by 1929 when the ore never paid. **Every building is gone**; what remains is an overlook
  above cliff mines, foundations, and the story. Look, don't take — artifacts are protected (ARPA).
  Legend says the rush was fed by tales of Ute elder Old Weaselskin's lost gold (label as legend).
- **A world-famous nerd fact:** Tuckers Tunnel (~1 mi south at 11,000 ft) is the **world type locality
  of the mineral theisite** — first described from this obscure prospect (mindat.org).
- **Runlett Park:** vast high meadow under Runlett Peak/Granite Peak sightlines; the road's upper bends
  give the big look back down on the reservoir.
- **Cave Basin/Dollar Lake overlook:** arguably the best non-technical viewpoint in the district.
- **Fall:** Beri's aspens (FS's own selling line) + hunting season traffic (GMU 751 — strong inference,
  60-sec confirm on CPW's GMU map at build).
- Middle Mountain CG at the bottom (24 lakefront sites at the Pine inlet) = the natural basecamp.
